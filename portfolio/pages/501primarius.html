Better Work with Primarius 1.0

"We hope that with UI improving suggestions you give us, we can attract more users to use the online ordering system."

Time

Sep. - Dec. 2017

Type

academic project / group work

My Work

background research / semi-structured interviews / affinity wall / UI improvement suggestions & report

* For our client's privacy concern, I will use either "client" or "food bank" to indicate it instead of using its real name.

Problem

The initial expectation of our client, a food bank, is to get some suggestions on improving the current UX/UI design in OOS, to attract more users to use it.

However, we recognized later that as the platform was a 3rd party software, it could not be changed immediately & directly by our client. That promoted us to explore more possibilities in assisting in solving the online ordering problem through other methods.

Discoveries

A background research and interviews with both staffs in the food bank and agencies' representatives who purchased items from our client, were conducted.

Key Insights

Customers' Diversified Behavior

There was a huge diversity in purchasing behavior among our customers. This therefore made it difficult to generalize them as a group.

Multi-Platforms Support

Though doing a good job in client communicatio, it was expected to provide more supports in various platforms to help online customers better.

More about Online Ordering

Customers usign online ordering system wanted the system to be more flexible, user-friendly and GUI wise. Even technical staffs took time to get familiar with it.

Recommendations

1. Improving Training & Communications

Internal Communications

We suggest that internal communication among different departments could be improved to work better for users' concerns and share existing but siloed expertise regarding the current syste.

a. Communication Through Online Platform

Customer service staffs and tech staffs can use tools like Slack or Google Sheet to record users' problems/feedbacks and solve problems together asap.

b. Communication Through Regular Meetings

Departments can set up regular meetings about reporting and solving users' problems with respect to the online ordering system. Meeting results can also be compiled into a Q&A document for later references.

Customer Trainings

We recommended our clients to increase their effort in providing diversified trainings for their customers.

a. Creating Online Tutorial

Client could create a simpler, more effective and accessible tutorial online. For instance, a video series could be created to give users a better idea about how to use the online ordering system.

b. Taylored Educational Courses

An in-person educational course taught by experienced staffs could help increase the tech skills of customers especially those with little proficiency, which could take their anxiety away that went with learning a new tool.

c. Dealing with Internet/Computer Access

Clients could develop or grow awareness around addtional methods of using the system for those agencies without computer or easy internet access.
